Meet the Martin Tax & Advisory Team

Our team of professionals brings decades of experience in tax strategy, accounting, payroll, and business advisory solutions designed to help your business grow.

Troy Martin, CPA

Founding Partner

Troy brings over 35 years of experience helping business owners minimize taxes and maximize long-term value through strategic planning and wealth integration.

Troy Martin doesn’t just interpret the tax code—he leverages it as a strategic tool for wealth creation and business growth. With over 35 years of experience advising business owners, executives, and affluent families, Troy has built a reputation as a trusted partner who delivers clarity, confidence, and measurable results in an increasingly complex financial world.

High-performing individuals and business leaders seek Troy’s expertise not only to minimize tax exposure but also to unlock opportunities that others overlook. His proactive approach focuses on advanced income tax and estate tax planning, succession strategies, mergers and acquisitions, and value maximization for closely held businesses. Troy’s ability to integrate tax, business, and personal wealth strategies sets him apart as a guide for clients who want more than compliance—they want vision.

Troy is deeply engaged in learning the intricacies of each client’s goals and enterprises. Whether navigating the sale of a company, orchestrating a generational wealth transfer, or structuring a business for sustainable growth, he brings the technical depth and practical insight to drive outcomes. Clients value his candor, his ability to simplify complexity, and his commitment to protecting and building their legacies.

With extensive experience in industries including medical, dental, manufacturing, retail, real estate, construction, agriculture, and beauty and spas, Troy has become a versatile advisor who understands the unique challenges and opportunities of diverse markets. He is particularly passionate about teaching clients how to transform financial data into actionable strategies that elevate performance.

Beyond his role at Martin Tax & Advisory, Troy is owner of several other business ventures including; Elite Esthetics Academy, (an esthetics school and med spa in Draper Utah) and Renegade Rentals, (an equipment rental company) with multiple locations in Utah. This entrepreneurial perspective allows him to connect with business owners not just as an advisor, but as a fellow operator who understands what it takes to scale and sustain a business.

When he’s not advising clients, Troy embraces a life of adventure—whether it’s on a Harley Davidson, a dirt bike, or exploring the outdoors through fly fishing, diving, playing golf with family and friends, and traveling. His drive for new experiences mirrors his professional philosophy: growth comes from embracing challenges and pursuing opportunities with purpose.

Brady Martin, CPA

Partner & Tax Manager

Brady is a founding partner of Martin Tax & Advisory and is committed to being a trusted advisor for his clients in every stage of life and business. He believes the real value of an advisor comes from long-term relationships built on trust — the kind where clients know they can call him not only for tax planning and business strategy, but also when the unexpected happens.

Brady takes the time to understand each client’s unique situation, goals, and family dynamics so he can provide advice that truly fits their needs. He views himself as a teammate to his clients, always keeping their best interests — and their families’ best interests — at the center of every decision. His goal is to build relationships that span years and generations, built on trust, consistency, and genuine care. He works closely with business owners across industries such as construction, real estate, and manufacturing, specializing in flow-through entities, tax planning, business structuring, and outsourced CFO services. With a strong understanding of the tax code and broad knowledge across most industries, Brady and his team are equipped to serve clients in nearly any field. He loves the challenge of complexity and enjoys continually learning — whether it’s mastering new tax strategies or understanding the evolving industries his clients operate in. Brady is passionate about helping clients build and preserve their wealth while saving on taxes, providing strategies that not only solve today’s problems but also create long-term financial advantages. Brady earned both his bachelor’s and master’s degrees in accounting from Utah State University, where he competed on the track and field team in the 400 meters, earned NCAA All-American honors and won multiple Mountain West Conference Championship event titles. Outside of the office, Brady enjoys time with his wife, Breann, and their son. He loves golf, riding his dirt bike, and the gym. Most weekends you’ll find him with family or friends — whether it’s on the golf course, riding in the mountains, or chasing his son around the yard.
