What We Do

Tax Compliance

Our tax compliance services ensure your business or personal finances are always in full alignment with federal, state, and local regulations. We provide comprehensive preparation and filing for income taxes, sales taxes, and payroll taxes. By identifying potential liabilities early and staying ahead of regulatory changes, we help you reduce audit risks and avoid costly penalties. Our proactive approach streamlines your compliance processes, so you can stay focused on growth with peace of mind.

Tax Planning

Tax planning is a critical component of financial success. We work closely with individuals, families, and businesses to develop year-round strategies that minimize tax liabilities while supporting your financial goals. From investment structuring and charitable giving to retirement planning and entity optimization, our team creates customized plans that adapt with your evolving needs. Effective planning means you don’t just react to tax events — you stay ahead of them.

Business Structure & New Business Set-Up

Starting a business is exciting — and the right foundation is critical. We help entrepreneurs and startups choose the most advantageous legal structure, whether it's an LLC, S Corp, C Corp, or partnership. Our team handles state and IRS registrations, helps develop an accounting system, and advises on tax implications from day one. With our guidance, your new business can launch efficiently, avoid early mistakes, and be structured for sustainable success and compliance.

Business Succession Planning

Preparing for the future of your business means building a strong succession plan today. Whether you're planning for retirement, a leadership transition, or unexpected events, we guide you through strategies to preserve value, maintain operations, and ensure a smooth transfer of ownership. Our approach includes valuation analysis, tax-efficient transfer structures, and alignment with your long-term personal and business goals. We help you leave a legacy — not uncertainty.

CFO/CEO Consulting and Support

Executives need more than reports — they need insights. Our CFO and CEO consulting services provide high-level strategic support tailored to your industry and stage of growth. We assist with budgeting, forecasting, cash flow management, and long-term financial planning. Whether acting as a fractional CFO or supporting an internal leadership team, our expertise helps drive profitability, streamline operations, and inform executive decisions with clarity and confidence.

QuickBooks Setup and Consulting

QuickBooks is a powerful tool — but only when configured properly. We help businesses set up QuickBooks for optimal performance, ensuring your chart of accounts, invoicing, expense tracking, and reporting are all aligned with your operations. Our consulting services cover ongoing troubleshooting, custom reporting, and staff training. Whether you're new to QuickBooks or need a cleanup, we make sure your system works for you, not the other way around.

Bookkeeping and Payroll Services

Accurate bookkeeping and timely payroll are the backbone of business operations. Our team provides full-service bookkeeping, including account reconciliation, financial statement preparation, and cash flow tracking. We also handle payroll processing, direct deposits, tax filings, and year-end reporting. With our support, you’ll gain reliable financial data, meet all deadlines, and stay compliant — freeing up your time to focus on growing your business.
