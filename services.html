Tax Compliance

Why It Matters: Tax compliance isn’t just about filing on time — it’s about reducing risk. Inaccurate or late filings can lead to IRS scrutiny, penalties, and audits that disrupt your business or personal finances. A professional tax compliance partner ensures your obligations are met accurately, on time, and with confidence.

What We Do: We manage the full tax filing lifecycle, including preparation of federal, state, and local returns for individuals, businesses, estates, and trusts. Our team tracks changing tax laws to ensure every deduction and credit is properly applied. We also offer year-end reporting, estimated payments, and representation for audits, all while maintaining clean records and documentation.

Benefits: Peace of mind, reduced audit risk, and confidence that your returns are fully compliant with current tax law.

Tax Planning

Why It Matters: Without strategic tax planning, you could be leaving thousands of dollars on the table. The tax code is designed with incentives — but only if you plan ahead. Proactive planning reduces your liability, eliminates surprises at tax time, and aligns your strategy with both short and long-term financial goals.

What We Do: We build personalized tax strategies for both individuals and businesses. This includes quarterly check-ins, income timing, deductions optimization, retirement contribution planning, entity structure evaluation, and tax-loss harvesting.

Benefits: Reduced tax liability, optimized cash flow, and a proactive approach that prevents end-of-year stress.

Business Structure & Setup

Why It Matters: Your business entity affects everything — your tax rate, liability, how you get paid, and future expansion. Choosing incorrectly at the start can limit growth or create unnecessary risk.

What We Do: We guide you through choosing between LLCs, S-Corps, C-Corps, partnerships, and sole proprietorships. We handle state registration, EIN acquisition, tax elections, and help with payroll and accounting setup.

Benefits: Reduced liability, maximized tax efficiency, and a streamlined launch.

Business Succession Planning

Why It Matters: Without a succession plan, even a thriving business can collapse. Whether transitioning to family, partners, or a buyer, clear planning preserves value and ensures operations continue smoothly.

What We Do: We assist with valuations, ownership transfer, buy-sell agreements, estate planning, and tax planning to help transition business leadership securely.

Benefits: Minimized tax liability, preserved legacy, and smoother transitions.

CFO/CEO Consulting

Why It Matters: Financial decisions drive growth, but many companies can’t justify a full-time CFO. With fractional consulting, you gain strategic insight at a fraction of the cost.

What We Do: Forecasting, budgeting, profitability analysis, cash flow planning, and executive-level strategy — without hiring in-house.

Benefits: Data-driven growth, better pricing models, and improved decision-making.

QuickBooks Setup & Consulting

Why It Matters: Poor bookkeeping setup leads to constant errors. QuickBooks done right saves hours and headaches later.

What We Do: Initial setup, chart of accounts, integrations, data migration, cleanup, and ongoing support.

Benefits: Accurate financials, faster closings, and clean records year-round.

Bookkeeping & Payroll

Why It Matters: Accurate books and timely payroll are essential to compliance, employee satisfaction, and clean tax filings.

What We Do: Full-cycle bookkeeping, payroll runs, tax filings, reconciliations, and reporting.

Benefits: On-time paychecks, reliable books, and audit-proof records.

Business Succession, Mergers & Acquisitions

Why It Matters: We work closely with clients to develop comprehensive succession plans that protect business value, minimize tax exposure, and align with long-term goals. For those considering mergers, acquisitions, or sales, we offer due diligence support, valuation analysis, tax-efficient structuring, and deal advisory services to help you make informed decisions and achieve optimal outcomes.

What We Do: At Martin Tax & Advisory, we help business owners navigate the complex financial and strategic considerations that come with transitioning or transforming their business. Whether you are preparing for retirement, passing ownership to the next generation, or exploring a merger or acquisition opportunity, our advisors provide the insight, structure, and planning necessary to ensure a smooth and financially sound process.

Benefits: Our goal is simple — to provide clarity, confidence, and continuity during times of change, helping you preserve the legacy you’ve built while securing the financial future you envision.
